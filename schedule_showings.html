About 95 West Street, Toronto, Ontario M3C 1T5

#Welcome to this extra large 2 bed 2 bath in a stunning bay-fronted limestone townhouse on the border of Clinton Hill and Bedford Stuyvesant. The kitchen features top-of-the-line stainless steel appliances, ample counter space and storage. The kitchen is south facing, filling the room with gorgeous sunlight. In the front of the unit you will find two gorgeous bedrooms. The primary offers an en-suite bathroom that includes a double vanity. There is a washer and dryer in the unit and options for private roof terrace. Located on a quiet tree-lined street, just moments away from the best that Clinton Hill and Bedford Stuyvesant have to offer. Some of the acclaimed restaurants within a few blocks of 132 Clifton Place include Speedy Romeo, Mekelburgs, Aita, and Place des Fetes to name a few! Enjoy local favorites such as coffee shops, bakeries, and boutique shops, or enjoy the beautiful tree-lined streets of these historic neighborhoods.
